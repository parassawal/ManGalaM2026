About the Hackathon

The Tech Fest Hackathon is a 24-hour coding marathon where developers, designers, and innovators come together to build software solutions from scratch.

Participants will push their limits, utilizing modern tech stacks to create functional prototypes that address given themes or open innovation challenges.

Why Participate?

  • Showcase your coding and system design skills.
  • Experience intense collaboration and teamwork under pressure.
  • Catch the eye of tech recruiters and industry judges.

Eligibility

Open to All Students

Students from any college or university are welcome to participate.

2 - 4 Members

Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 and maximum of 4 members.

Tech Required

Bring your own laptops, chargers, and any required hardware.

Event Timeline

1

Idea Registration & Shortlisting

Teams submit their initial project idea and tech stack online. Top ideas will be invited for the offline hackathon.

2

Kick-off & Theme Announcement

The 24-hour timer begins. Specific tracks/themes and APIs may be revealed.

3

Overnight Coding Phase

Continuous development, mentorship rounds, and midnight snacks.

4

Code Freeze & Final Pitch

Development stops. Teams deploy their projects and present a 5-minute pitch + demo to the judges.

Rules & Guidelines

  • All code must be written during the 24-hour period. No pre-built projects allowed.
  • Use of existing open-source libraries, frameworks, and APIs is highly encouraged.
  • Teams must submit their GitHub repository link before the code freeze deadline.
  • Plagiarism or presenting someone else's work as your own will result in instant disqualification.
  • Judges’ decisions regarding code quality and functionality are final.

Judging Criteria

Functionality & Execution 30%
Innovation & Creativity 25%
Code Quality & Architecture 20%
UI/UX & Design 15%
Pitch & Presentation 10%
