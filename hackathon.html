About BOLT 2.0

Ready to innovate, build, and compete with the best? BOLT 2.0 is the flagship Hackathon of Tech Fest ManGalaM 2026.

Teams will tackle specific real-world problem statements, ranging from Disaster Management to Blockchain verification, building functional prototypes under pressure.

Why Participate?

  • Solve Real-World Problems: Work on problem statements that matter.
  • Showcase Your Skills: Prove your system design and coding capabilities.
  • Exciting Prizes & Recognition: Win big and catch the eye of industry judges.

Problem Statements

Smart Rural Education

Develop a solution to improve access to quality education in rural areas using digital tools, offline learning support, or low-cost technology.

Disaster Management

Build a system that helps in early warning, communication, and coordination during natural disasters to save lives and resources.

Smart Parking Finder

Create a platform that allows users to find, reserve, and navigate to available parking spaces in real-time.

Smart Queue Management

Design a system to reduce waiting time by managing queues efficiently in places like hospitals, banks, or public offices.

Fake Profile Detection

Develop a secure system using blockchain to verify identities and detect fake or duplicate profiles on digital platforms.

Health Symptom Checker

Build an AI tool to give people quick access to preliminary health advice and help them locate nearby healthcare facilities.

Format & Eligibility

2 - 4 Members

Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 and maximum of 4 members.

Round 1: Online Qualifiers

The online qualification round will take place on 24th March.

Round 2: Offline

The final round and presentations will be held completely offline during the fest.
