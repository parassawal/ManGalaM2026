About the Challenge

The IoT Arena challenges you to build hardware-software integrated prototypes that solve real-world problems through connectivity and automation.

From smart home systems to industrial IoT devices, bring your microcontrollers, sensors, and code to the main stage and demonstrate your working prototype to the judges.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation: Uniqueness of the hardware solution.
  • Execution: Functionality and physical build quality of the prototype.
  • Connectivity: Efficiency of data transfer and software integration.

Eligibility

Team Size

Teams can consist of 1 to 4 members. Collaboration across different technical disciplines is heavily encouraged.

Hardware Required

Teams must bring their own microcontrollers (Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP32, etc.) and sensor kits.
