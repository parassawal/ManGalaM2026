About the Event

The Shark Tank Event at ManGalaM 2026 is a premier platform for innovative thinkers and aspiring entrepreneurs to present their startup ideas before a distinguished panel of judges (the Sharks).

Participants will have the unique opportunity to pitch their concepts, explain their business models, and convince the investors why their idea deserves funding, incubation, and recognition.

Purpose of the Event

  • Encourage entrepreneurship and leadership among students.
  • Promote innovative and sustainable solutions to real-world problems.
  • Give students a high-profile platform to showcase ideas and gain mentorship.

Who Can Participate

MGM College Exclusive

Participation is open strictly to students of MGM College.

2 or 4 Members

Teams must consist of exactly 2 or 4 members.

Originality Required

Participants must present a completely original idea or startup concept.

Event Format

1

Round 1 – Idea Submission

Teams submit their Startup idea, Problem statement, and Business concept online through the registration portal.

2

Round 2 – Shortlisting

The most promising ideas are carefully evaluated and selected by our preliminary screening committee.

3

Round 3 – Final Pitch

Shortlisted teams present their idea live on stage in front of the Sharks.

  • 5 Minute Presentation: Pitch your vision clearly.
  • 3 Minute Q&A: Answer questions from the judging panel.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Ideas must be absolutely original and innovative.
  • Teams must register before the strict deadline.
  • Teams must strictly consist of 2 or 4 members.
  • Pitch presentation should not exceed 5 minutes.
  • Participants must bring their presentation (PPT/PDF).
  • Plagiarized ideas lead to instant disqualification.
  • Judges’ decision will be final and binding.

Judging Criteria

Innovation & Originality 25%
Feasibility of Idea 20%
Market Potential 20%
Business Model Robustness 15%
Quality of Presentation 10%
Q&A Handling 10%
