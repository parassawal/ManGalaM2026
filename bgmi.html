About the Tournament

Welcome to the Mangalam Tech Fest BGMI Tournament, the ultimate Battlegrounds Mobile India eSports showdown.

Squads from across colleges will drop into Erangel and Miramar to prove their tactical dominance, raw aim, and team coordination in high-stakes custom rooms.

Tournament Highlights

  • Compete across multiple classic maps including Erangel and Miramar.
  • Face off against the best squads in the region.
  • The Grand Finals will be broadcasted live on the main stage screen!

Eligibility

MGM Students Only

Strictly restricted to students currently enrolled at MGM institutions. Non-MGM players will be disqualified.

4 Player Squads

Strictly 4 players per squad. Substitutes must be registered beforehand.

Mobile Only

Strictly mobile devices only. Emulators and tablets/iPads are heavily restricted.

Tournament Format

1

Offline Qualifiers

All registered squads must be physically present at the venue. Squads will be placed into custom rooms to battle for final slots.

2

Offline Grand Finals

The top 16 squads clash live on stage in a 4-match series to crown the ultimate BGMI champions of Mangalam.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Custom Room IDs will be shared exclusively with the Squad Leader via WhatsApp 15 minutes prior.
  • All players must have downloaded the required maps beforehand.
  • Use of emulators, triggers, or iPads is strictly prohibited.
  • Teaming up with other squads will result in an immediate permanent ban.
  • Use of any third-party hacks, GFX tools, or modified clients will lead to instant disqualification.

Points System

Placement Points (Chicken Dinner = 10pts) 60% Weightage
Kill Points (1pt per Finish) 40% Weightage

Tie-Breaker Rules

In the event of a tie, the team with the higher total finishes will be placed higher. If kills are also tied, the placement of the final match will decide.
