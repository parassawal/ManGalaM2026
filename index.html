About Tech Fest

Welcome to ManGalaM 2026, the flagship technical festival of MGM College! A celebration of technology, innovation, and engineering brilliance.

Over the course of the fest, thousands of brilliant minds from across departments converge to test their technical prowess, collaborate on cutting-edge projects, and compete for massive prizes across 13 distinct arenas. From high-octane eSports and Robotics to deeply technical Hackathons and Parliament simulations, ManGalaM 2026 is designed to push your limits.
