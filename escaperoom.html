About the Challenge

Welcome to the Escape Room Competition, where strategy, intelligence, and coordination are the keys to success.

This year features exciting, immersive Mahabharata-themed challenges. Teams will be locked in and must work together to crack codes, interpret ancient clues, and solve sequential puzzles to break out before the clock runs out!

Skills Tested

  • Logic & Problem-Solving: Decipher complex riddles under pressure.
  • Observation: Find hidden clues scattered throughout the room environment.
  • Team Coordination: Communicate effectively to combine findings and unlock the exit.
