About the Challenge

The 3D Model event is a premier showcase for digital artists, industrial designers, and architects to present their most innovative 3D creations.

Whether it's a character sculpt, an architectural visualization, a mechanical assembly, or an abstract concept, participants will present their models, workflows, and rendering techniques to an expert panel.

Judging Criteria

  • Topology & Modeling: Cleanliness and efficiency of the 3D mesh.
  • Texturing & Lighting: Quality of materials, UV mapping, and final render presentation.
  • Creativity: Originality of the concept and design execution.

Eligibility

Solo or Duo

Participants can enter as individuals or form teams of maximum 2 members.

Software Allowed

Any industry-standard 3D software is permitted (Blender, Maya, 3ds Max, SolidWorks, ZBrush, etc).
