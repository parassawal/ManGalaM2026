About the Session

The Youth Parliament is a simulation of the legislative process designed to empower students with an understanding of parliamentary procedure, critical thinking, and public speaking.

Participants will assume the roles of Ministers, Parliamentarians, and Opposition Leaders to debate current affairs, propose bills, and defend policy frameworks in an intense, formal setting.

Skills Highlighted

  • Debate: Articulate arguments with clarity and confidence.
  • Policy Making: Draft and propose realistic legislative bills.
  • Diplomacy: Form alliances and negotiate outcomes.
