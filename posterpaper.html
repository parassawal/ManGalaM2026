About the Event

The Poster & Paper Presentation is an innovation-focused event where students can showcase their technical research, projects, and futuristic ideas.

Whether you prefer the visual impact of a well-designed poster or the rigorous depth of a whitepaper, this platform allows you to present your findings to industry experts and peers.

Event Categories

  • Poster Presentation: A visual summary of your project/idea.
  • Paper Presentation: A formal presentation of your technical research paper using PPTs.
  • You may choose to participate in Either or Both categories.

Eligibility

Open to All Years

Students from 1st Year (FE) to Final Year (BE) across all branches are actively encouraged to participate.

Individuals

You can participate and present your research as an individual author.

Groups (Max 3)

You may also form a group. The primary author + up to 2 co-authors are allowed (Max 3 members total).

Rules & Guidelines

  • Topics must be related to Science, Technology, Engineering, or innovative concepts.
  • For Papers: Presentations should be strictly within 8-10 minutes followed by a 2-minute Q&A session.
  • For Posters: Standard poster dimensions apply. Posters must be printed and brought to the venue by the participants.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited. Research papers must be original or appropriately cited review papers.
  • Judges’ decisions regarding scores and winners are final.
